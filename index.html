Welcome

During my initial job search after graduating in May 2014, it became apparent that social media was growing and there was a need to stay connected as often as possible. Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram and others. These are just a small number of services that people use to stay in touch and to connect.

All of these services, different usernames, and e-mail addresses, can be hard to organize. Many years ago I had some personal business cards printed, and I found it difficult to print long web addresses, such as the one for LinkedIn.

That's where this website comes in. It is an easy portal to my contact information, and other social media pages.
